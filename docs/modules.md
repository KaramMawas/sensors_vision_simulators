# Modules

## Camera Parameters
Exposure photography parameters.

## Depth Camera
LiDAR and time-of-flight depth simulation.

## KD-Tree
Spatial indexing and nearest-neighbor search utilities.

## Photogrammetry
Lens distortion, Thin lens simulator, Pin-hole projection, Scale ambiguity, Epipolar, Structure from Motion (SfM) concepts.

## Structured Light Scanning
Pattern projection and capturing simulation.

## Terrestrial Laser Scanning
ToF and Phase-shift simulators.
